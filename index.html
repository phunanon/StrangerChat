Rules

  • Respectful behavior: No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • Age and consent: No obscene or inappropriate content or conversations without the expressed age & consent of your chat partner. No users under 18 may engage in sexual conversation - both chat partners are liable for this.
  • No under-18s in NSFW: No users under 18 may use the NSFW feature.
  • No spamming: This includes self-promotion without your partner's consent.
  • Listen to moderators: Comply with moderator instructions and decisions.
  • Identity: Fictional identity is fine, but not impersonation of someone else.
  • Reporting: Report rule violations via the bot or directly to moderators.
  • Meaningful reports: Do not report someone for false or trivial reasons.
  • Prohibited content: No discussion involving illegal activities, obscenities, or content prohibited by moderators.
  • Compliance with Terms: Comply with the latest Terms of Service.
Violation of any of these rules may result in the suspension or termination of bot access, as outlined in the Terms of Service.

Terms of Service

These terms and conditions ("Terms") govern use of the anonymous matchmaking bot called StrangerChat ("the Bot") by you ("the User") provided by Patrick Bowen ("the Provider"), along with moderation staff appointed by him ("Staff"). By using the Bot, you agree to abide by these Terms. If you do not agree with any part of these Terms, you may not use the Bot.

1. Data Collection, Use, and Privacy

The Bot collects the following personal information from Users:

  • Discord ID
  • Discord tag
  • Expressed gender and gender preferences
  • Declared age in years (13-17, then 18+)

All interactions on the Bot are recorded and monitored.

Personal information is stored securely in a database on a cloud server, and used solely for the purpose of providing anonymous conversations, matching Users based on their gender preferences, and assisting Staff with moderation. Conversation messages are stored in private Discord channels only accessible to the Provider, and in the Direct Messages of each chat partner.

Reported conversations are evaluated by OpenAI technology, and the summarised ruling, along with select quotations, are shared with Staff in a private Discord channel.

Messages containing double-digits, and surrounding messages, are also evaluated by OpenAI technology to compare ages with what was previously declared to the Bot, and the ruling and select quotations are shared with Staff in a private Discord channel. The Provider does not share User data with any other parties unless relevant to moderation. All data shared to OpenAI is not tied to any Discord user, and is textually stripped of Discord tags.

Data is retained for as long as the User continues to have access to the Bot, and can be deleted upon request.

3. User Conduct, Moderation, and Termination

Users must follow the rules provided by the Bot and refrain from any prohibited actions. Breaking these rules or engaging in behavior outside of the Bot that violates these Terms may result in the temporary suspension or permanent termination of the User's ability to use the bot. This includes circumventing the rules by using alternative accounts. Account sharing is prohibited.

The Bot employs OpenAI Artificial Intelligence (AI) via a Large Language Model (LLM) with the ability to:

  • Evaluate reported conversations and determine whether the reported user was inappropriate, obscene, or sexual.
  • Automatically mute the reported User.
  • Automatically and permanently move the reported User to the NSFW section if it is expilictly evident that within the conversation that they are aged 18 or above.
  • Evaluate messages containing double-digits which substantially differ from the age the user previously declared, to determine if it is likely an age, what that age is (extraction), and considering surrounding messages to determine false-positives. If all these conditions are met, the User has their age challenged, which must be resolved with Staff.

AI decisions are made without prior human oversight, but are fully audited and reversible by the Provider and Staff.

4. Dispute Resolution

Users can report conversations or contact the Provider or Staff directly to raise concerns or disputes. All concerns are handled confidentially, and discussions are not disclosed to other parties.
