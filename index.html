Rules

  • Respectful behavior: No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • Age and consent: No obscene or inappropriate content or conversations without the expressed age & consent of your chat partner. No users under 18 may engage in sexual conversation - both chat partners are liable for this.
  • No under-18s in NSFW: No users under 18 may use the NSFW feature.
  • No spamming: This includes self-promotion without your partner's consent.
  • Listen to moderators: Comply with moderator instructions and decisions.
  • Identity: Fictional identity is fine, but not impersonation of someone else.
  • Reporting: Report rule violations via the bot or directly to moderators.
  • Meaningful reports: Do not report someone for false or trivial reasons.
  • Prohibited content: No discussion involving illegal activities, obscenities, or content prohibited by moderators.
  • Compliance with Terms: Comply with the latest Terms of Service.
Violation of any of these rules may result in the suspension or termination of bot access, as outlined in the Terms of Service.
