  • Discord ID

Personal information is stored securely in a database on a cloud server, and used solely for the purpose of providing anonymous conversations, matching Users based on their gender preferences, and assisting Staff with moderation. Conversation messages are stored in private Discord channels only accessible to the Provider, and in the Direct Messages of each chat partner.

Reported conversations are evaluated by OpenAI technology, and the summarised ruling, along with select quotations, are shared with Staff in a private Discord channel.

Messages containing double-digits, and surrounding messages, are also evaluated by OpenAI technology to compare ages with what was previously declared to the Bot, and the ruling and select quotations are shared with Staff in a private Discord channel. The Provider does not share User data with any other parties unless relevant to moderation. All data shared to OpenAI is not tied to any Discord user, and is textually stripped of Discord tags.

Data is retained for as long as the User continues to have access to the Bot, and can be deleted upon request.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I do "predator catching"?

Where you pretend to be younger than you actually are on the bot in order to trick adults into flirting with you, leading to their ban.

While we do not frown upon predator catching itself, we do ask that you:

  • Tell the bot your actual age - if you've already put an age lower than your own please let us know.
  • Don't say that you're younger than you are on the bot - you'll have to be unspecific and do this in DMs.

This is because we have an automatic AI-powered system in place which keeps minors and adults largely unmixed. When you pretend to be younger than you actually are, the bot rightly catches you as an adult pretending to be a minor, or a potential minor that has lied about their age. However, if ever you do see an adult acting inappropriately when they believe you are a minor then please report it, even if it's in DMs.

We also cannot accept minors who purposefully "catch pedos" - this is a severe safeguarding issue.

Why don't you support anonymous voice calls?

Anonymous calling would require 2× the amount of code and complexity that the bot has right now, plus it's virtually impossible to moderate, and fewer people would use the feature than people expect.

This is because typically a bot can only be in one voice channel at a time, so I would require a fleet of bots to handle simultaneous connections.

Why do I get "Your message could not be delivered"?

You need to enable your DMs for this server in its privacy settings.

Why is matching taking a long time?

The bot's user base is not spread equally around the globe - for example if you're in a time zone which doesn't usually have many active Discord users, not all of your waking hours will have many people to chat with.

However, there are things you can do to improve your match speed, even when it's quiet:

  • Use /gender and tell the bot to match with anyone rather than a particular gender - this considerably shortens your wait time.
  • You can see how much of an impact this has by using /wait-times.
  • Get a Premium subscription or trial, which gets you chatting 2× as fast as non-Premium users - /premium.

How does your moderation system work?

When you use /report, it is evaluated by one of OpenAI's GPT models. It summarises the conversation for human moderators, and automatically decides if the reported person has been inappropriate. If both parties haven't been inappropriate, it will take instantaneous action by muting the reported person for three days.

There's also automatic moderation of sensitive topics such as child abuse material.

We also enforce our rules in DMs - if we are sent a report via server ticket of misbehaviour then you are liable to be punished.

Why does the bot ask my age?

We operate an age split to keep minors safer and keep adults happier. You are required to tell the bot your real age before you can use it. You will lose access to the bot if you are suspected to have lied.

  • Users aged 13-17 are matched with users aged 13-17
  • Users aged 18+ match with 18+

If you get into a situation where you feel you have to lie about your age, don't - it is better to not mention your age at all, or disconnect, than for you to lose access to the bot.

Why can't I match by age, region, interests, etc?

Each time a new match filter is added, it increases the wait times. Imagine there are 100 people to talk to at any given time - splitting by age means you now only match with about half of them.

Splitting by gender makes this dramatically worse, because of the many pairing permutations (M4F, F4M, M4M, F4F, etc).

If we added more filters, the number of available people would shrink further, and wait times could grow to hours or even days. We're just not as big as Omegle was, for example.

Why are there so many pedos on this server?

I have seen people often complain that this server has "so many pedos", or that it's "unsafe", and it's disheartening.

The reason it seems like there's so many pedos is because I've ensured that there's a culture on this server of ratting them out.

I've ensured that:

  • you can use /report and that it is always investigated
  • you can post in #screenshots to warn others
  • you can do #ban-proposals
  • you can open a ticket and report somebody to staff
  • there's a system to detect people lying about their age on the bot
  • moderation history is catalogued to corroborate past and present behaviour
  • alts are tracked

We have banned more than two thousand members over the lifetime of this server, including pedos, NSFW accounts, gore posters, hackers, spammers, and more.

Do you ever tell anybody who spoke with who on the bot, or what we talk about?

No, never. If any Moderators do find out, through individual reports, or guesswork, etc, we will never tell anybody who you were speaking with, or that you were speaking with someone, or what you were talking about.

You, your chat partners, and me are the only three humans (other than Discord staff) that can read your entire conversations. AI reads reported conversations, and Moderators only get glimpses through screenshots and /report AI-generated summaries.

Why is the server so "dead"?

If you read the activity message of the bot, you will see how many people have used it in the past hour, and how many messages are being sent per minute on average.

On the bot there could be one message every second and yet the server itself could be silent.

The server exists for people to find and use the bot. Don't be surprised that most activity is there rather than in the server itself.
