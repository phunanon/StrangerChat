Rules

  • Respectful behavior: Treat others with respect and kindness at all times, unless in jest and the partner knows it is in jest. However harassment, hate speech, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • Obtain age and consent: No obscene or inappropriate content or conversations without the expressed consent of your chat partner. Users under the age of 18 are not considered to be able to express consent to users of the age 18 or over.
  • No spamming: While the bot itself does attempt to mitigate spam, do not try to bypass these measures. This includes self-promotion without the consent and interest of your partner.
  • Listen to moderators: Comply with their instructions and decisions.
  • True identity: You are never required to reveal your true identity, but you must not impersonate someone else.
  • Report violations: If you feel your partner has violated any of these rules then you must report it via the bot or directly to moderators.
  • Meaningful reports: Do not report someone for no reason, or because you simply do not like them.
  • Compliance with Terms: You must comply with the Terms of Service, and adhere to any updates or changes made to these Terms.
  • Prohibited Content: Users must refrain from sharing or engaging in discussions involving illegal activities, obscene content, or any other content deemed inappropriate by the moderators.
Violation of any of these rules may result in the suspension or termination of the user's account, as outlined in the bot's terms of service.
